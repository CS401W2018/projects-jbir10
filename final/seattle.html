About Seattle

Seattle, named after Chief Sealth of the Suquamish tribe, was settled in 1851 by the Denny Party and is located on the eastern shore of Puget Sound, offering stunning views of water, mountains, and forests. The city spans 83.9 square miles and boasts over 200 miles of combined freshwater and saltwater shoreline, perfect for outdoor activities like hiking and kayaking. Seattle's mild climate features average temperatures ranging from 37°F in winter to 77°F in summer, making it ideal for year-round exploration. Visitors can enjoy the vibrant cityscape, breathtaking views, and its rich cultural and historical heritage.

My top 3 things to do in Seattle

There's a lot of things to do in Seattle, some of them are recommended in specific weather. I have been lucky to visit seattle in summer and winter season. During summer is quite hot but it's always good to see the sun and see the colorful city. While in winter, it's cold and grey outside which makes it the perfect time to go around the city or try new food places. Anyways my three favourite things to do in Seattle is hiking, cruising around the city, and trying out new foods.

Hiking

Seattle’s hiking trails take you through beautiful forests, mountain ranges, and lakes, offering views of Mount Rainier and the Cascades.

Foods

Seattle is famous for its coffee culture and fresh seafood, especially salmon, enjoyed in many local dishes.

Views

Seattle offers breathtaking views of Mount Rainier, and the city skyline, with iconic attractions like the Space Needle for sightseeing.
