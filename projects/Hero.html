Sergio 'Kun' Aguero

Sergio Aguero was a professional soccer player from Argentina.
He started his senior career in a home town club called Independiente.
He then got a transfer to a major european club Athletico Madrid.
In 2011 he got transferred to Manchester City, I started watching
Kun ever since, he is the highest goal scorer for the club at 260
goals in 390 matches. When he was past his prime playing days, he
went to Barcelona, also a footballing giant club, played few games
before he was forced to retire due to health reasons.

He was one of the best Strikers in the world in his prime.
