Engagement Questions

The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?
It specifies the URL where form data is sent for processing.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?
It defines how the data is sent, either as GET or POST.

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?
POST sends data privately in the body, while GET sends data openly in the URL, visible to users.
